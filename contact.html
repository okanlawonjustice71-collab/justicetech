Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline depends on the project scope. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline after discussing your requirements.

Yes, I offer various support packages depending on your needs. This can include technical support, updates, and maintenance services to ensure your project continues to perform optimally.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I have experience working with clients from various countries and can accommodate different time zones through flexible scheduling and communication.
